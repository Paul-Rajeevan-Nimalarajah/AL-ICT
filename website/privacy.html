Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AL ICT Notes Hub ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at alict.paulrajeevan.com.

2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information from visitors browsing the website. However, the following may be collected through third-party services:

  • Google Forms: If you submit a form (e.g., to submit notes or report an issue), the information you provide (name, email, message) is collected by Google and stored in Google Drive. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages: The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server log information. Refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • Cookies: This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics tools of its own.

3. How We Use Information

Any information submitted via Google Forms is used solely to respond to your inquiry, add tuition advertisements, or review note submissions. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites (e.g., WhatsApp, Google Drive, tuition class pages). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies when visiting them.

5. Data Security

We do not store any personal data on our own servers. All form data is managed by Google's secure infrastructure. We take reasonable steps to ensure the security of the resources shared on this platform.

6. Children's Privacy

AL ICT Notes Hub is intended for A/L students (typically aged 17+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@paulrajeevan.com or via the Contact Page.
