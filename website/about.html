About AL ICT Notes Hub

A free, community-driven platform built to help A/L ICT Tamil Medium students across Sri Lanka succeed in their exams.

Our Mission

AL ICT Notes Hub was created with a simple but powerful goal: make quality ICT study resources freely available to every Tamil Medium A/L student in Sri Lanka, regardless of their school or location.

We believe that access to education should never be limited by geography or resources. By building a central hub for notes, past papers, and tuition connections, we aim to level the playing field for all students preparing for the G.C.E. Advanced Level ICT examination.

What We Offer

  • Verified Tamil Medium unit notes (Units 1–14)
  • Past papers archive from 2011 to 2024
  • Model & term papers from schools
  • Free tuition advertisement directory
  • Community note contributions
